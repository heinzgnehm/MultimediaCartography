About

The Atlas of Chicago is the result of the project for the coursee of Multimedia Cartography at ETH.
The aim of the project is to diplay statistical data about crime social-economic factor and ethnicity over Chicago.
The Atlas is meant to be consulted by citiziens , urban planner or simply by people interested in the topic.
