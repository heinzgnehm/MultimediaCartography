About

The Atlas of Chicago is the result of the project for the course of Multimedia Cartography at ETH.
The aim of the project is to display statistical data about crime, social-economic factor and ethnicity over Chicago.
The Atlas is meant to be consulted by citizens, urban planners or simply by people interested in the topic.

Source of the data:

Crime and and socio economic indicators - City of Chicago Data Portal.
Ethnicity - United States Census 2010.
